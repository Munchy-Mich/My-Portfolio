TruthCheck

Combating Misinformation in Nigeria

Role: Product Owner/Manager
Duration: 5-day sprint (April 2025)
Team Size: 24 cross-functional members

Project Overview

Led the development of TruthCheck, a web application designed to empower Nigerian citizens with quick information verification capabilities. The solution focused on delivering a robust Quick Check Tool that enables users to verify news, images, and claims through an intuitive interface optimized for entry-level smartphones.

Industry: Information Verification & Media Technology

Target Users: Nigerian citizens with varying digital literacy levels

Primary Goal: Combat misinformation affecting public health and democratic processes

Technical Focus: Mobile-first design for entry-level smartphones

Problem Statement

Nigeria faces a critical misinformation crisis affecting public health, security, and democratic processes. With high mobile penetration but varying digital literacy levels, citizens lack accessible tools to verify information authenticity. The challenge was particularly acute during breaking news events where speed and accuracy requirements often conflict.

Key Challenges:

  • High mobile penetration with low digital literacy levels
  • Lack of accessible information verification tools
  • Speed vs. accuracy conflict during breaking news events
  • Limited access to credible fact-checking resources
