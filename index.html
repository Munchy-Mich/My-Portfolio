About Me

As an Associate Product Manager with a passion for innovation and user experience, I bring a unique blend of strategic thinking and creative problem-solving to every project. My journey in product management began with a deep curiosity about how technology can solve real-world problems and improve people's lives.

My approach centers on gathering detailed requirements, facilitating cross-functional collaboration, and delivering solutions that genuinely address user needs. I believe in data-driven decision making combined with empathetic user research to create products that make a meaningful impact.

Through comprehensive product management training and hands-on projects, I bring strong capabilities in user research, workflow analysis, stakeholder management, and cross-functional coordination. I excel at translating complex requirements into actionable strategies while maintaining a data-driven yet empathetic approach that keeps users at the center of every decision.

I'm particularly passionate about emerging technologies and their potential to transform industries. Whether it's exploring the applications of AI in product development, understanding the implications of new regulations on product strategy, or identifying opportunities in untapped markets, I'm always looking for ways to push the boundaries of what's possible.

Education & Professional Development: I hold a Bachelor of Science in Applied Microbiology and Brewing from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, which provided strong analytical and research skills. Recently, I completed the Learnable Product Management Internship from Genesys (2025), gaining comprehensive training in modern product management methodologies, user experience design, and agile development practices. I also earned Project Management Fundamentals certification from the British Project Management Academy (2019) and Proficiency in Management from the Nigerian Institute of Management (Chartered) (2019). This combination of scientific education and specialized training has equipped me with both analytical rigor and practical management skills essential for effective product management.
