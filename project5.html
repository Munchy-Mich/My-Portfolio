URGENT 2KAY

Revolutionizing Bill Payment Through Smart Bundling

Role: Product Manager
Duration: 4 weeks (April 21 - May 16, 2025)
Team Size: 24 members

Project Overview

URGENT 2KAY is a fintech web application that enables users to bundle multiple bills and financial needs into a single request. Upon acceptance, the platform automatically disburses payments directly to service providers, removing intermediary cash handling and providing complete transparency.

Duration: 4 weeks development cycle

Team Size: 24 cross-functional members

Beta Testing: 5+ external users

Live Application: https://urgent2kays.netlify.app/

Problem Statement

Bill payment remains fragmented and inefficient, particularly when individuals need financial assistance across multiple expenses. Traditional payment apps only facilitate direct person-to-person transfers without visibility into fund allocation, creating friction in family and relationship dynamics around financial support.

Solution

URGENT 2KAY addresses these challenges through smart bundling technology that allows users to:

  • Aggregate multiple bills into single payment requests
  • Provide complete transparency in fund allocation
  • Enable direct payments to service providers
  • Remove cash handling intermediaries
  • Maintain relationship trust through verified transactions

Target Users

Primary Users

Bill Owners:

Students, partners, dependents seeking assistance with multiple expenses

Bill Sponsors:

Parents, partners, relatives willing to cover others' expenses

Secondary Users

  • Service Providers: Utilities, educational institutions, merchants
  • Expense Managers: Users coordinating financial responsibilities across relationships

Streamlined Request Management

  • Organized bill bundle creation with detailed breakdowns
  • Branded request delivery system

Direct Provider Payments

  • Instant disbursement upon request acceptance
  • Real-time payment confirmation notifications

Relationship Management

  • Profile creation for different financial relationships

  • Contribution tracking and acknowledgment features

Transparency Tools

  • Visual fund allocation breakdowns
  • Expense verification through provider connections
  • Spending category analysis and trend reporting
  • Budget planning and forecasting capabilities

Key Learnings and Iterations

User Feedback Integration

During beta testing, users requested enhanced notification controls to balance urgency without creating pressure. We implemented customizable notification settings and introduced "gentle reminder" options for sponsors.

Technical Challenges

Provider API integrations required more extensive error handling than initially planned. We developed robust fallback mechanisms and improved user communication around payment status updates.

Team Collaboration

Leading a cross-functional team through a compressed timeline required daily standups and clear milestone definitions. The structured weekly goal approach proved effective for maintaining momentum while ensuring quality deliverables.

Future Roadmap

Based on initial user feedback and market research, identified expansion opportunities include:

  • Medical expense bundling capabilities
  • Investment contribution coordination
  • Educational savings plan integration
  • Institutional support system partnerships
